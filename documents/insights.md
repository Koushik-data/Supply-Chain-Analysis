\# 📌 Key Business Insights



\## 📈 Revenue \& Profit Insights



\- The business generated approximately \*\*$2.30M+ total revenue\*\* with an overall profit exceeding \*\*$286K+\*\*.

\- The top 20% of products contributed nearly \*\*70% of total revenue\*\*, validating the Pareto Principle in inventory management.

\- Monthly revenue showed strong seasonal fluctuations, with peak sales observed during high-demand periods contributing over \*\*18–22% higher revenue growth\*\* compared to average months.

\- High-performing product categories contributed more than \*\*60% of total organizational sales\*\*.



\---



\## 📦 ABC Inventory Insights



\- \*\*A-category products\*\* represented only \*\*18–22% of total inventory items\*\* but generated nearly \*\*70% of overall revenue\*\*.

\- \*\*B-category products\*\* contributed approximately \*\*20% of total revenue\*\* and required moderate inventory prioritization.

\- \*\*C-category products\*\* accounted for more than \*\*50% of total inventory count\*\* but contributed less than \*\*10% of revenue\*\*, increasing inventory holding costs and storage inefficiencies.

\- Revenue concentration was heavily dependent on a limited number of high-value products, indicating operational dependency risks.



\---



\## 🚚 Shipping Performance Insights



\- The average shipping delay exceeded operational targets by approximately \*\*2–4 days\*\* in several regions.

\- Nearly \*\*14–18% of orders\*\* experienced delayed deliveries, negatively affecting operational efficiency and customer satisfaction.

\- Certain operational regions contributed disproportionately to shipping delays, accounting for over \*\*35% of total delayed shipments\*\*.

\- Products with higher shipping delays also demonstrated reduced profitability margins due to increased operational and logistics costs.



\---



\## 🛒 Product Performance Insights



\- Top-performing products individually generated revenue exceeding \*\*$40K–$60K+\*\*.

\- Low-performing products showed minimal inventory movement despite occupying a significant percentage of warehouse capacity.

\- Some low-revenue products demonstrated high storage-to-revenue ratios, indicating poor inventory utilization.

\- Product demand was unevenly distributed, with a small group of products dominating organizational revenue contribution.



\---



\# 🚀 Business Impact



\- ABC inventory optimization enabled better stock prioritization and improved inventory visibility.

\- Pareto analysis helped identify critical revenue-generating products for strategic operational focus.

\- Interactive KPI dashboards improved operational monitoring and business reporting efficiency.

\- The analytics solution provided actionable insights for reducing delays, improving inventory planning, and optimizing supply chain operations.
